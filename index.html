Project 3

Two maps below are both about temperatures influences on tea area in different provinces of China. I experiment this theme with:

(1) Proportional symbol map + Choropleth map

(2) Bivariate map

Finding that the first one shows the number comparison between tea plantation area more obvious visually than the second one. The second one emphasizes more on relationship between temperature and tea plantation areas, but require viewer's time and thinking to interpret. So the first one is chosen to be the final outcome for this theme.

The theme is inspiried by learning plants in Biological Geography 338, which helps me to gain a background on tea plants first. I also apply the knowledge of linear regression calculation from Stat 301 to show the relationship between tea plants growth and temperature variation.
